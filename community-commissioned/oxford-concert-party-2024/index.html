Oxford Concert Party

A musical organisation (see music pages) who were commissioned to create workshops at several elderly people's homes. They invited a variety of artists to join them. These images were taken by residents at Lake House Care Home, Adderbury, Oxfordshire. The general theme was 'Food' and in particular for these images 'patterns and food'.
