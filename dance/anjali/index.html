Anjali Dance Company is a pioneering professional contemporary dance company, developing the creativity of people with learning disabilities. Over 10 years of vital dance making, the Company has performed at major arts venues throughout the UK and abroad. The Company also has a Youth Dance Company, runs classes for children and adults, and provides Education and Outreach programmes which are led by dancers themselves.
